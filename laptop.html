Laptop Stand

Engraved Wooden Laptop Stand

2023

I designed a wooden laptop stand with an engraving as a personal project in order to organize my workspace.

Power tools were used to design the stand from wood pieces. The engraving was designed and then sketched before being engraved by hand.
