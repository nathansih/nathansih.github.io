Socks Booklet

Graphic Design Final Project

2023

I designed and produced a booklet with images of myself looking through socks for a final project in a graphic design course. Socks were chosen to make the project more playful, as well as to work with depth and texture in photography.

Adobe Photoshop was used to edit and compile photos. Adobe InDesign was used to design the booklet layout.
