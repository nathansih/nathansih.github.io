Airless Basketball

Wilson Sporting Goods Co.

2024

Designed and engineered both public and unreleased “Factory of the Future” projects for the innovation of basketball manufacturing, including the Wilson 3D-printed Airless Basketball. Participated and contributed in all aspects of the product development lifecycle.

Intensive physical and digital prototyping and testing of products. Physical prototyping was done with various additive manufacturing techniques of 3D printing, injection molding, reaction injection molding, and rotational molding. Digital computer software programs included SolidWorks, Rhino 3D, Grasshopper, and nTop, with exposure to NX and Catia.
